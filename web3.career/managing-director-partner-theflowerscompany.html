Company Background

Flowers Equity Partners Management, LLC (FEP) is a subsidiary of The Flowers Company, LLC, specializing in alternative investments. FEP has made notable investments and exits in Blockchain technology, Web3, Real Estate, Cannabis, and FX trading assets since 2020. Our core values of Integrity, Passion for Excellence, Accountability, Teamwork, and Entrepreneurship guide us as an organization and an employer.

Summary


We want to partner with a candidate who feels their potential is untapped and desires to build something significant. If you’re only seeking a paycheck, this isn’t it. We are mission-focused. The Managing Director role is a Partner-track, senior level position within the firm. Leveraging multiple years of private equity investing and value creation experience, an MD will manage and help drive Flowers’ efforts across the investment life cycle, including investment thesis generation, deal sourcing, deal execution, debt, and equity capital raising, portfolio management, and exit of portfolio investments. Additionally, the MD will further expand the firm's reach by building trust-based relationships with key parties (e.g., investors, bankers, portfolio company executives, investment targets, advisors, and limited partners) while serving as a trusted partner to the firm's senior leaders.

The ideal candidate for this position is a self-starter with experience raising capital from accredited, high-net-worth individuals and family offices. Flowers seeks a hardworking individual with strong financial and interpersonal skills to recruit investors for FEP's projects and maintain investor relationships.
